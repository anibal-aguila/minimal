PROMPT-H Information Technology Educational, Trade and Service Ltd.

Prompt Ltd. was founded in 1989 for ICT trainings, software and hardware development, and computer service. The founders are highly qualified experts on ICT, delivering lectures for university students. Prompt Ltd. is specialized for software and hardware development, mainly on the field of Technology Enhanced Learning Systems (dynamical e-learning system (LMS, LCMS, CMS), portal engine based on RDBMS) and digital security, alarm and observational systems. The company boasts innovative trainers, creative IT engineers who working together to deliver the latest knowledge with the newest, computer based methods and high performance IT technology. On this basis the company has carried on several R&D projects with success both at national and European level.

The tradition of Prompt is planned and stable growth. The company will continue operating according to this tradition in the future, always bearing in mind that growth does not only mean quantity, but also quality, especially in the field of efficiency, effectiveness and good organization. In the long term only a customer-oriented, strong and successful company will be able to meet the challenges of market competition. These principles are behind the fact that Prompt has a wide professional and business partnership in the Middle Hungarian Region with multinational corporations, with research institutes and higher educational institutes.
